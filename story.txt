Two twins named Alex and Conner Bailey travel into a magical world full of the fairytales that their parents havve told them since their childhood. After facing numerous obstacles and meeting quite a few villains from fairytales, they finish colllecting all the spell items that can help them go back home. However, they surprisingly face the Evil Queen, Snow White's stepmother. She steals all the items and uses them for her own purpose. Also, the twins are captured by the queen and locked in a castle. Then, the Fairy Godmother, who turned out to be Alex and Conner's grandmother, saved them and took them home.
